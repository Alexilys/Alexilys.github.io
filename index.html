HOW TO USE:
Please write the percentage in the format '00.00'.
If you have two stats of the same type (fx All Units HP and Army HP), add them together. Leave out stats not relevant to your troop type!
ATTACK SPEED
ATK SPD is one of the most valuable stats to have on a chip - but only if your ATK SPD is higher than your opponents. If you have a chip with ATK SPD, I'd recommend asking an experienced player for advice. This calculator does not account for ATK SPD!
PLEASE NOTE!
These calculations are only estimates! If you're in doubt, it's always better to ask an experienced player, since they often have insights that this calculator can't give you. Have fun!
